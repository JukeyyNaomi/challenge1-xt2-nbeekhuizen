About ITS

Elon Musk is the founder and CEO of SpaceX. SpaceX designs, manufactures and launches advanced rockets and spacecraft. In September 2019, Elon Musk unveiled the company's Interplanetary Transport System (ITS). The most powerful missile ever built. A spaceship is designed to transport a minimum of 100 people to the Red Planet per flight. It's 2030.103 people paid $ 200,000 for a ticket to travel to Mars and Elon Musk asked the Hague University of Applied Sciences to help.
